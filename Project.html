Sea Animal Crackers

Main Page About Our Team

The crackers were inspired by the beautiful sea life. Most animal-shaped products include land creatures or only fish and we wanted to embrace the rest of the world. The sea life that the crackers are based on ranges from dolphins to jellyfish, each one of them being more exotic than the next. To preserve the exoticness of sea animal's existence, parts of our profits are going to be donated to local charities dedicated to the preservation of living beings on Earth. Also to help the environment and animals, we are a Non-GMO and cruelty-free company that is striving for ways to make our buildings and factories safe for the habitat. We also use ingredients grown locally like fresh paprika or wheat flour from farm-grown wheat to not only take care of the planet but the consumers as well. The packaging of the product itself is also biodegradable to ensure that the plastic waste of the environment is reduced and helps prevent injuries to local animals.



The crackers are based on the photos displayed and many other sea life forms.
